Barriers we help families navigate

Lack of benefit awareness

Families may not know what reparations, insurance, survivor, or funeral resources exist.

Complex applications

Grief often overlaps with forms, deadlines, eligibility requirements, and repeated follow-up.

Criminal-justice stress

Families may need clear explanations, emotional support, and consistent presence during hearings and trials.

Our Commitment

Stand The Gap is committed to providing compassionate, trauma-informed support while measuring outcomes responsibly. As we continue to grow, we will publish updated impact information using verified, de-identified data to protect the privacy of every family we serve.

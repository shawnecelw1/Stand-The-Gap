# Stand The Gap

**Stand The Gap** provides crisis response, grief navigation, and community-based support designed to bridge gaps between emergency systems and lived experience.

---

## Our Mission
To provide compassionate, culturally responsive support during moments of crisis, loss, and transition.

---

## What We Offer
- Crisis & grief navigation
- Community-based support
- System-bridge services
- Resource connection & advocacy

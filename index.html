Mission

Stand The Gap exists to provide immediate, trauma-informed crisis support and grief navigation to individuals and families impacted by homicide and violent loss.

Vision

A future where no family affected by homicide stands alone and crisis response includes compassion, clarity, and culturally responsive care.

Core Values

  • Presence over performance
  • Restoration over relief
  • Dignity and cultural humility
  • Faith in action
  • Collaboration

Why Stand The Gap Exists

Stand The Gap was born from lived experience and the belief that families deserve guidance, presence, and support long after the immediate crisis.

Stand The Gap was born from lived experience. After the homicide of my twin brother, my family and I were suddenly thrust into a world of grief, trauma, court proceedings, investigators, and unanswered questions.

While law enforcement focused appropriately on identifying and prosecuting those responsible, there was little guidance for the family left behind.

Stand The Gap was created to be that support for others. Through crisis case management, advocacy, resource navigation, and compassionate follow-up, we help families move from immediate crisis toward stability, healing, and hope.

About

Stand The Gap provides immediate, trauma-informed support after violent loss—delivered with dignity, cultural responsiveness, and professional accountability. Our role is to stand in the gap between tragedy and long-term healing—working alongside counties, law enforcement, and victim services. We do not replace therapy or existing victim services. We strengthen them by filling the critical gap immediately following violent loss.

Mission Statement

Stand in the Gap exists to provide immediate, trauma-informed crisis support and grief navigation to individuals and families impacted by homicide and violent loss. We meet people in their most vulnerable moments—offering presence, stability, advocacy, and compassionate guidance—so they are not left to navigate grief, systems, and shock alone. Rooted in faith, integrity, and professional excellence, our mission is to restore dignity, strengthen resilience, and support healing through every stage of loss.

Vision Statement

A future where no family affected by homicide stands alone, and where crisis response includes compassion, clarity, and culturally responsive care alongside public safety efforts.

Core Values

  • Presence Over Performance – We show up before we solve.
  • Restoration Over Relief – We focus on long-term stability, not temporary fixes.
  • Dignity & Cultural Humility – Every family is honored in their grief.
  • Faith in Action – We serve with compassion, integrity, and accountability.
  • Collaboration – We strengthen—not replace—existing systems.

Founder's Story

Stand The Gap was born from lived experience. After the homicide of my twin brother, my family and I were suddenly thrust into a world of grief, trauma, court proceedings, investigators, and unanswered questions.

While law enforcement focused appropriately on identifying and prosecuting those responsible, there was little guidance for the family left behind. We did not know what steps to take next, who to contact, what resources were available, or how to navigate the overwhelming emotional and practical challenges that followed.

During one of the most difficult periods of our lives, we found ourselves searching for support that did not exist in a coordinated or accessible way. We needed someone to help bridge the gap between crisis and healing—someone who understood both the emotional impact of homicide and the complex systems families must navigate afterward.

Stand The Gap was created to be that support for others. Our mission is to ensure that no family affected by homicide has to face the aftermath alone.

Through crisis case management, advocacy, resource navigation, and compassionate follow-up support, we help individuals and families move from immediate crisis toward stability, healing, and hope.

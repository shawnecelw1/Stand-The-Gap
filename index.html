Services

Rapid Crisis Response

Post-incident outreach, stabilization, and coordinated support following homicide or violent death.

Grief & Trauma Stabilization

Trauma-informed grief support for individuals and families, with short-term follow-up.

System Navigation & Advocacy

Support accessing resources, referrals, and victim-centered services—without families carrying it alone.

Consultation & Training

Optional support for counties and departments on grief-informed, culturally responsive response practices.

Who we serve

Families impacted by homicide or violent death, communities disproportionately affected by violence, and Minnesota counties, cities, and law enforcement agencies.

Why it matters

Early, coordinated intervention reduces long-term trauma impacts, strengthens community trust, and improves outcomes for families and systems alike.

For Counties & Law Enforcement

A professional partner that strengthens post-incident response while ensuring families receive compassionate, clinically grounded support.

Options: 90-day pilot • on-call response • retainer • fee-for-service • outcome reporting
